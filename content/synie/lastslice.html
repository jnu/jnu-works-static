PIE Project (The last slice of it.)

Hello! Here is the final, for now, iteration of my work visualizing Carl Darling Buck's Dictionary of Selected Synonyms. I say "for now" because, as you'll see, there is an enormous amount of data missing from this widget. The unpleasant realities of having to pay my rent prohibit me from spending any more time on it for now. But I do hope the project delights! If you aren't sure what you're looking at, have a look at my series of blog posts about this project for more details. Thanks for viewing!
By Mr Joe Nudell in 2013.
